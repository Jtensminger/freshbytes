Why it Works

Frozen bananas, a straightforward and popular treat, are perfectly suited for automation. Our robots handle dipping, topping, and serving, ensuring consistency and eliminating the need for human labor in the process.

As plant-based, low-calorie desserts gain popularity, frozen bananas meet the demand for healthier dessert alternatives. With various topping options, from nuts to dark chocolate, it appeals to a broad range of dietary preferences.

Compact, Mobile, and Scalable

Our kiosk model is designed to operate in high-traffic areas with minimal space requirements. It's scalable across multiple locations, offering a sustainable business model with low overhead costs.

Interactive Experience

Customers engage with the kiosk through a user-friendly touchscreen, making selections for dips and toppings while watching the robots prepare their treat in real-time, offering a novel and engaging customer experience.
